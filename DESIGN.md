# NOD Visual System & Guardrails (v4.4)

## Strict Tailwind Color Tokens
- **Background Default:** `bg-void` (#050505) — solid dark workspace.
- **Structural Cards & Containers:** `bg-monolith` (#0c0c0c) — brutalist matte cards.
- **Luxury Highlight Accents:** `text-champagne` or `border-[#d4af37]/20` — muted gold styling.

## Typography Architecture
- **Font Serif (`font-serif`):** Main headers, numerical scores, and ranking boards.
- **Font Sans (`font-sans`):** Navigation items, small meta fields, and descriptions.

## Zero-Period Policy (Politique du Point Final)
Standalone status indicators, labels, flags, and button descriptions must NEVER terminate with a period character.
- **CORRECT:** "FILE VIDE" | "AUCUN REC" | "VOTE ENREGISTRÉ"
- **INCORRECT:** "FILE VIDE." | "AUCUN REC." | "VOTE ENREGISTRÉ."
